Bible notify is a free and open-source, offline, privacy-friendly daily Bible verse notification app for Android.

- Randomly sends you a notification from a selection of 150+ inspiring Bible verses.
- Set the time of day Bible Notify will send you the verse notification each day â€”right down to the minute, if you so desire.
- Simply tap on the notification to read the chapter in which the verse is found in the app's built-in Bible chapter reader.
- Features the RKJV version (English) and the AELF translation (French).
- Bible Notify is fully functional offline.
- No tracking or data collection whatsoever.

A desktop version of Bible Notify is also available for Windows, Linux, and macOS (see https://biblenotify.github.io for downloads).
